FOUNDERS OF MMS FOR GIRLS & BOYS

Huma Rehman (Co-Founder & Academic Head)

Huma Rehman, an MPhil in Education, is a visionary leader with extensive expertise in developing innovative educational strategies. Her experience in the education sector spans over several years, where she has consistently worked towards enhancing teaching methodologies and creating engaging learning environments. Huma’s academic journey and hands-on experience allow her to design programs that cater to each student's intellectual and emotional needs, making MUFLIHOON Model School a beacon of excellence.
